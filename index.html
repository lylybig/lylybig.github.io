Research Topics

My strong research interests lie in deep generative models and its application in computer vision, geometric deep learning, graph neural networks, and AI for Science.

Besides, I am also engaged in developing robust learning models against data sparse and distribution shifts in real life, in general, such as long-tail learning, imbalanced learning and few-shot learning.
